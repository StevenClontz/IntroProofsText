Activity 5.5.1. Practice with Antisymmetric.

For each of the following relations, determine if it is antisymmetric.

(a)

Let \(A=\{1, 2, 3\}\) and \(R=\{(1, 1), (1, 2), (1, 3), (2, 1), (3, 1)\}\text{.}\)

(b)

Let \(A=\{1, 2, 3, 4\}\) and \(R=\{(1, 1), (1, 3), (2, 2), (3, 2), (3, 3)\}\text{.}\)
