To prove
  • Reflexive.
    Let \(x\in A\text{.}\) Show \((x, x)\in R\text{.}\)
  • Symmetric.
    Assume \((x, y)\in R\text{.}\) Show \((y, x)\in R\text{.}\)
  • Antisymmetric.
    Assume \((x, y)\in R\) and \((y, x)\in R\text{.}\) Show \(x=y\text{.}\)
  • Transitive.
    Assume \((x, y), (y, z)\in R\text{.}\) Show \((x, z)\in R\text{.}\)
