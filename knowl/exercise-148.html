Exercise 3.

Define \((a, b)\leq (c, d)\) if \(a\leq c\) and if \(a=c\) then \(b\leq d\text{.}\)
  1. Prove or disprove \(\leq\) is a partial order on \(\mathbb{Z}\times\mathbb{Z}\text{.}\)
  2. Prove or disprove \(\leq\) is a total order on \(\mathbb{Z}\times\mathbb{Z}\text{.}\)
Note, this is often called the lexicographic or dictionary order.
